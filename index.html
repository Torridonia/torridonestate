The cottages

Lochside Cottage

Direct loch views — the most scenic position on the estate.

Gardeners Cottage
Families
Adjacent to the walled garden. Comfortable, private, and well suited for families.
Follow the estate road — Gardeners Cottage sits just past the walled garden on your left. Follow the track around and you'll come to it directly.
Corry Cottage
Very private
Set furthest back on the estate — the most private of the four.

Stables Cottage
2–4 guests
Converted stable block with character interiors. Good for small groups.

Breakfast
Served in the dinning room from 8:00–9:00 AM. Each morning we put together a warm plate — typically two scrambled eggs with feta, spinach and chorizo, served with cherry tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, a pain au chocolat and a pastry. There's also granola, cereal, fresh milk, coffee and tea.

Please let us know in advance if you have any dietary requirements or restrictions — we're happy to adapt.
