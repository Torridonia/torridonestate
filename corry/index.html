Wood-fired hot tub

1
Fill the tub
Use the hose to fill the tub with water. This takes 1–2 hours — stay nearby to prevent overflow.
2
Light the fire
Once the tub is filled, light the fire in the burner. Keep the cover on to help the water heat up efficiently.
3
Heat the water
Let the fire burn for 2–3 hours, checking the water temperature as needed.
4
Enjoy
When the water reaches your preferred temperature, let the fire burn out naturally, remove the lid, and take a dip.
Safety
  • Water can become very hot — always test before entering
  • Supervise children and non-swimmers at all times
  • Never leave the fire unattended — ensure it is fully extinguished after use
  • Stay hydrated, especially on warm days

Waste & recycling

General waste
Use the black and white bin bags provided in the cottage. Our team handles all waste at the end of your stay — feel free to use the bins for any excess during your visit.
Recycling
Place all recycling in the large blue bin. Note that glass cannot go in this bin.

Glass disposal — two options:
  • Drop it at the glass recycling bin at the Torridon Inn
  • Leave it in the cottage — our team will take care of it on departure

Safety & house rules

Please never leave unattended
  • Fires or open flames
  • Candles
  • Washing machine or dryer
Always switch off electrical devices when not in use.
Plumbing — please do not flush
  • Sanitary products
  • Napkins or paper towels
  • Cooking fat or oil
This is a rural property on a septic system. These items cause serious blockages — thank you for your understanding.
